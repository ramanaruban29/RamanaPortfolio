About me

I am a Computer Science graduate from the Department of Computer Science, University of Jaffna. I played a pivotal role as a Co-investigator for the World Bank-funded Agriculture Products Management System. This endeavor resulted in the creation of an E-commerce platform using the dynamic Laravel framework, which was subsequently transformed into a sleek mobile app and made available on Google Play.

Notably, my undergraduate research, which produced a Masked Face Recognition system, earned a distinction in recent academic evaluations. Additionally, I pioneered the development of a dataset on Paddy Leaf Diseases, aiming to enhance agricultural practices and community welfare. this was done during my research assistant tenure at Department of computer science, university of jaffana.

During my 6-month internship at Codelantic (Pvt) Ltd., I excelled as a Trainee Software Engineer (Backend - Java) with an exceptional A+ grading. Throughout, I focused on microservice architecture, Spring Boot API creation, and validation. I optimized APIs, developed Gateway services, and conducted JUnit5 unit testing. Additionally, I integrated microservices into SonarQube for code quality analysis and managed production logs using Kubernetes. Proficient in creating Postman collections and implementing Selenium Automation, I honed my expertise in backend Java development.

During my one-and-a-half-year tenure as a Trainee Staff Assistant at Sampath Bank, I gained valuable experience in various aspects of banking operations. Throughout my training period, I honed my skills in customer handling, marketing, and canvassing banking products. This hands-on experience equipped me with a comprehensive understanding of customer service dynamics and marketing strategies within the banking sector.
